# 👨‍💻 About Me

I'm a *Data Analyst* passionate about turning raw, messy data into clear, actionable insights that drive business decisions.

🎓 B.E. in Computer Engineering from *Don Bosco College of Engineering*

✅ Worked as a *Data Analyst – Implementation & Reporting at Tech Mahindra Business Services*, where I built KPI dashboards and drove data quality & governance for enterprise/financial-services clients.

During that role, I:

- 📊 Built and maintained interactive KPI dashboards in Power BI and Excel
- 🧹 Performed data validation, profiling, and root-cause analysis across large-scale datasets
- 🐍 Automated recurring reports using Python (Pandas), reducing manual effort
- 🗂 Maintained data dictionaries, glossaries, and lineage documentation for governance compliance
- 🤝 Collaborated with data engineering, implementation, and third-party partner teams to deliver analytical projects
- 🎓 Led stakeholder training and change management during BI dashboard rollouts

I enjoy digging into data, spotting patterns others miss, and translating numbers into decisions non-technical stakeholders can act on.
